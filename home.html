What is the definition of ICT?

Information and communication technology, abbreviated as ICT, it covers all technical means used to handle information and aid communication.



What are the basic Functions of a Computer?

The basic functions of a computer are entering data, processing them and producing processed data (information). Input


what is the difference between data and information?

Data comprises raw, unprocessed facts that need context to become useful, while information is data that has been processed, organized, and interpreted to add meaning and value.
